💡 Study Tips for Secondary Students

Tackling the B1 Listening test requires strategy, just like preparing for a big project at school. Keep these tips in mind:

  • Pre-reading is key: Just like skimming a textbook chapter before class, always read the questions and options before the audio starts. Know what you are listening for!
  • Beware of distractors: In multiple-choice questions, you will usually hear all three options mentioned. For example, someone might say, "I really wanted to play football, but it rained, so I went to the cinema." The correct answer is cinema!
  • Context Clues (Gap Fill): In Part 3, think about what type of word is missing. Is it a date? A number? A noun? It's exactly like filling in the blanks in your science lab notes.
